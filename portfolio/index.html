Sister Mary

This was a University project for the photoillustration degree, the concept was about to represent a character from Stephen's King book / The dark tower - The gunslinger. Sister Mary was the leader of the little sisters and was often referred to as 'Big Sister'. As a vampire she fed off of the blood and pleasure of humans to maintain a youthful human appearance. This project wants to show the process and transformation of a composition integrating some photos and making them part of a story with my point of view of this character.

  • Category: Photo manipulation
  • Date: April 2015

Manglares de papel

Logo creation for "Videosónica" an electronic arts festival at Museo la tertulia in Cali, Colombia.

Manglares de papel was a project for the Art & technology subject, where there was a mixture between the origami and the analogous interfaces, to represent an ecosystem based on the mangroves existing in the Colombian Pacific; This project was part of the international meeting of electronic arts, presented at the Museo la tertulia in Cali, Colombia.

The development of the logo was inspired by the folds of paper when origami is made, the symmetrical and geometric forms are part of what I transmit as a designer and the color palette supports the colors represented by the Colombian Pacific.

  • Client: Manglares de papel
  • Date: April 2015
  • Service: Logo creation

Antiproduct

This project was done for the Photo Illustration, its concept is about the anti-product, to be able to think and to develop an illustration of a product with the objective of deforming the functionality of the same one. Whenever we wash our hair, it is very fun to fill with foam and possibly to invent new hairstyles with it; We have the idea that the shampoo is healthy and that it will leave us a hair silky, bright and clean. The anti-concept I reflected in the illustration, is that when using that shampoo, which is yellow and usually has honey, it is the bees that will do the job of annoying, that the result of the same product is chaotic and That is as tangled as his own nature.

  • Date: April 2014
  • Service: PhotoIllustration
